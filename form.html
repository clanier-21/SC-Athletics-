Membership Details

If you want to support South Carolina athletics through GoGamecocks, there are 2 ways. The first is a membership of which the options are shown below. The second is a custom recurring or one time payment which allows for users to select how much they want to give and how often. The custom payment method does not come with the membership benefits.

Silver $1,000 Gold $2,000
  • Access to exclusive behind-the-scenes content.
  • Quarterly digital newsletters with player interviews and team updates.
  • Limited edition South Carolina Athletics NiL program merchandise.
  • Priority access to purchase single-game tickets.
  • Everything in Silver Level, plus:
  • Monthly virtual meet-and-greet sessions with athletes and coaches.
  • Exclusive early access to new merchandise releases.
  • Name featured on the NiL program supporter's wall at the stadium.
  • 10% discount on official team merchandise.
Diamond $2,500 Gamecock $3,250
  • Everything in Gold Level, plus:
  • Two complimentary tickets to a select home game.
  • VIP access to pre-game warm-ups and post-game press conferences.
  • Personalized shout-out during home games.
  • Invitation to an annual VIP appreciation event with players and coaching staff.
  • Everything in Diamond Level, plus:
  • Customized South Carolina Athletics NiL jersey with your name.
  • Private dinner with select athletes and coaching staff.
  • Exclusive invitation to away game watch parties with special guests.
  • Recognition as a Platinum Elite supporter in all official communications and on the team website.
